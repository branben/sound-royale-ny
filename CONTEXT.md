# Sound Royale — Domain Glossary

## Product

**Sound Royale** — A multiplayer music bingo game where producers compete head-to-head by completing bingo boards with audio tiles. Players upload samples, the game broadcasts them in real-time, and opponents race to match and claim tiles.

## Aesthetic

- **Visual direction:** Polished Jackbox — premium game-show energy with playful character. Clean, bold, flat colors on near-black. Minimal UI chrome. Custom vector illustrations (not doodles). TV broadcast presentation.
- **Not:** Dark tech, cyberpunk, synthwave, neon glow, glassmorphism, gradients.
- **Color system:** Per-player colors assigned at room join (Player 1 = red, Player 2 = blue, etc.). Player's color infuses their cards, tiles, avatar borders, and highlights throughout the UI.

## Roles

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Producer** | A player actively competing on the bingo board. Uploads audio, claims tiles, races for bingo. |
| **Spectator** | A non-competing viewer. Can vote on rounds when enough spectators are present (≥3 unlocks ranked mode). Future: can influence beat creation. |

## Game Mechanics

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Bingo Board** | 3×3 grid of genre tiles. First producer to complete 3 in a row wins. |
| **Tile** | A cell on the bingo board representing a music genre. States: empty, pending (uploaded, awaiting playback), complete (claimed). |
| **Round** | One turn where the active genre is announced, producers upload a beat for that genre, tiles are played, and spectators vote. |
| **Match** | A full game from lobby to bingo/winner. |
| **Genre** | Music category for a tile (phonk, trap, lofi, house, drill, rnb, etc.). |

## Player Identity

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Title** | Achievement badge earned by a producer (JACKPOT, SWEEPER, CHECKED_IN). Displayed on player cards and profile. |
| **ELO** | Rating score reflecting skill. Starts at 1200. |
| **Genre Performance** | Per-genre win/loss record shown as a radar chart in the player profile modal. |

## Social

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Discord Link** | Optional account linking. Shows Discord username and verified icon on player cards. |
| **Voting** | Spectators vote on the best beat each round. Ranked mode (≥3 spectators) makes votes count toward outcomes. |

## Future Concept

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Spectator Influence** | Spectators can affect beat creation (e.g., "remove all hi-hats," "add more reverb"). Winner earns coins. Not yet implemented. |
